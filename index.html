WHAT WE STAND FOR
THE THREE PILLARS
01

DEFEND

Every D.B.P member is trained to recognize bullying and step in. We place ourselves between the bully and the target. We do not look away. We do not walk past.

02

PROTECT

We follow up. We check in. We make sure the person who was hurt feels safe — not just in that moment, but every day after. Protection doesn't end when the incident ends.

03

INSPIRE

We do good deeds — not because someone told us to, but because we choose to. Random acts of kindness. Community service. Proving that kids can change the world.

SUPREME CHIEF — OLIVER VO

D.B.P was founded by Oliver Vo, age 10, from Ladera Ranch, California. Oliver has Tourette syndrome and knows firsthand what it feels like to be different, stared at, and misunderstood.

He founded BrightWing to send joy to kids with disabilities. He founded B.O.L.D to fight bullying undercover at school. Now he's going bigger — a worldwide physical organization to defend every bullied person on earth.

D.B.P is his biggest mission yet.
